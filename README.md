# 🛒 Madhav Ecommerce Sales Dashboard

An interactive **Power BI Dashboard** built to analyze and visualize the sales performance of Madhav Ecommerce. The dashboard provides key insights into revenue, profit trends, product categories, customer behavior, and payment preferences.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

The dashboard presents a comprehensive view of ecommerce sales data through the following KPIs and visuals:

### 🔢 Key Metrics (KPI Cards)
| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Sum of Amount (Revenue) | 438K |
| Sum of Quantity (Units Sold) | 5,615 |
| Sum of Profit | 37K |

---

## 📈 Visuals Included

### 1. 📅 Profit by Month *(Bar Chart)*
- Displays monthly profit trends across all 12 months (January – December).
- Highlights months with negative profit (losses) using a distinct color, making it easy to identify underperforming periods.

### 2. 🗺️ Top State *(Horizontal Bar Chart)*
- Shows the top-performing states by sales amount.
- States included: **Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Uttar Pradesh, Delhi**
- Madhya Pradesh leads with the highest sales volume.

### 3. 🍩 Sum of Quantity by Category *(Donut Chart)*
- Breaks down total quantity sold across three product categories:
  - 👗 **Clothing** – 63%
  - 💻 **Electronics** – 21%
  - 🪑 **Furniture** – 17%

### 4. 💳 Sum of Quantity by Payment Mode *(Pie Chart)*
- Shows the distribution of orders by payment method:
  - 💵 **COD (Cash on Delivery)** – 44%
  - 📱 **UPI** – 21%
  - 🏦 **Debit Card** – 13%
  - 💳 **Credit Card** – 12%
  - 📆 **EMI** – 10%

### 5. 👤 Profit by Customer *(Bar Chart)*
- Displays profit contribution by top individual customers.
- Customers shown: **Harivansh, Madhav, Madan Mohan, Shiva, Vishakha**

### 6. 📦 Sum of Profit by Sub-Category *(Horizontal Bar Chart)*
- Highlights which product sub-categories are the most profitable:
  - 🖨️ **Printers** – Highest profit
  - 📚 **Bookcases**
  - 👘 **Saree**
  - 💍 **Accessories**
  - 🪑 **Tables**

---

## 🎛️ Filters / Slicers

| Filter | Options |
|---|---|
| Quarter | Qtr 1, Qtr 2, Qtr 3, Qtr 4 |
| State | All / Individual States |

These slicers allow dynamic filtering of the entire dashboard for focused analysis.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- **Tool:** Microsoft Power BI Desktop
- **File Format:** `.pbix`
- **Data Visualization:** Bar Charts, Donut Chart, Pie Chart, KPI Cards
- **Interactivity:** Quarter & State-level slicers for dynamic filtering

## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Clone or download** this repository.
2. Open the `.pbix` file using **Microsoft Power BI Desktop**.
3. Explore the dashboard using the **Quarter** and **State** slicers to filter data dynamically.
4. Hover over any visual to see detailed **tooltips**.
